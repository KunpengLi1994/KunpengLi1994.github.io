About Me

I am a second-year PhD student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department, Northeastern University, advised by Prof. Yun Fu. My current research interests lie in computer vision, machine learning and deep learning. Prior to this, I obtained my bachelor degree from School of Electronic and Information Engineering, South China University of Technology, supervised by Prof. Xin Zhang and Prof. Lianwen Jin.
